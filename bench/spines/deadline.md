# Reasoning spine: deadline discipline (conditional)

Motivation (2026-07): the largest catastrophe cluster in the clean-run tail (about 10 of
24 misses at Brier >= 0.49) had one shape — "will INSTITUTION do ACTION by DATE" priced
0.72–0.92 and resolving NO: Mexico tariff (us 0.87, the SOTA teacher 0.05), TikTok
enforcement memo (0.72 vs 0.06), Venezuela decree renewal (0.72 vs 0.05), FCC enforcement,
CDC schedule label, a US continuing resolution. The teacher priced bureaucratic slippage;
we priced announcements. Loop-1 taught that GLOBAL caution just hedges (calibration up,
refinement down, net zero) — so this discipline is CONDITIONAL: it fires only on the
detectable pattern, and it prices mechanics, not pessimism.

## Deadline discipline (mandatory ONLY where it applies)

Apply when resolution requires a specific action or event to occur BY a deadline and it
has not yet occurred. Skip entirely for questions already settled by present facts or
where the deadline is not the binding constraint.

1. WINDOW ARITHMETIC first: from the resolution criteria, state the event window, the
   fraction already elapsed with the action still not done, and the time remaining. An
   action absent at X% of its window is evidence it finishes absent, roughly in
   proportion — say the number before adjusting it.
2. REMAINING STEPS: list the concrete procedural steps still required (drafting, votes,
   publication, signature, comment periods, printings). Each remaining step is an
   independent delay opportunity; two or more remaining steps with under six weeks left
   slips more often than it holds.
3. SLIPPAGE PRIOR: "scheduled", "expected", "on track", and official calendars are weak
   evidence — institutions miss self-declared deadlines at high base rates even when
   sincere. Anchor on how often this institution type completed this action type within
   such a window; adjust on completed steps, never on stated intent.
4. ASYMMETRY: the NO side of a by-deadline question requires no one to do anything.
   Weight that explicitly before setting your number.

This is not a mandate to be low: an action with every step complete and only a formality
remaining prices HIGH. The discipline is pricing the remaining mechanics.
