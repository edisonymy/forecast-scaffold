# Conditional research move: institutional action by deadline

Use this move only when the frozen router marks
`institutional_action_by_deadline=true`. Do not add any part of it to non-fired
questions.

1. Fetch the authoritative official docket, register, agenda, calendar, filing, or
   status page for the qualifying action. Record its status and the most recent completed
   step, with dates and direct sources.
2. Enumerate every remaining step required before the resolution criteria can be met.
   Name the responsible actor, dependencies, mandatory waits, scheduled meetings, and
   publication or implementation requirements for each step.
3. Do window arithmetic from the forecast as-of time to the resolution cutoff. Compare
   the time remaining with the dated sequence already completed and with observed
   durations for the same type of step.
4. Find institution-specific evidence on comparable deadlines: prior dockets, calendars,
   postponements, extensions, missed target dates, and late completions. Separate binding
   dates from announced, expected, or aspirational dates.

Return a compact research note containing the official status, remaining-step chain,
calendar arithmetic, comparable slippage evidence, dated sources, and unresolved factual
gaps. Then resume the ordinary forecasting workflow.
